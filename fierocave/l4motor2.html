Yard Surfing...

Here is the most complete list I have of what vehicles got the DIS 2.5l L4, including the Truck versions even though I don't know how compatible they might be with Fiero. I figure someone may need it in a pinch. (I thought the "Postal Vehicle" was funny so I left it.)

VIN R & U are all Front Wheel Drive cars or the Mid Engine Fiero.
All the R and U listed use the 1227748 ECM.

VIN U is shorter that R and have big problems using U to replace R.

VIN A & E are all Rear Wheel Drive Light Truck.
If these can be used in Fiero they will need considerably more work than R or U. You CANNOT use the truck ECM in Fiero.

Source: ACdelco
