ORDER PROCEDURE

The procedure for ordering is:

(1) Tell us what you want via the form below. You are under no obligation and NO PAYMENT IS SENT at this time. Simply describe what you want. Using the form insures we have sufficient information to provide a complete quotation. Please note all prices are US$ and payment must be in US$.

(2) We confirm your request and total price by return e-mail. (See Payment and Shipping.) If you are in agreement, let us know and send payment by check, money order, or bank transfer. We also accept credit card payment via PayPal (paypal.com) if you will include the PayPal fee. Our PayPal address is "fierosails@yahoo.com". We do not take credit card orders via phone. If you wish to use a credit card and are not familiar with PayPal we can send you a PayPal "invoice" so all you have to do is follow the step-by-step on-line instructions to use your card. If you want to utilize a bank transfer, please contact us. Our mailing address for checks or money orders is below. Please make checks payable to "Paul McKibben", NOT "fierosails.com".

Paul McKibben
4001 Centre Court
Norcross, GA 30092

(3) When your order is complete we will ship promptly upon receipt of payment.
