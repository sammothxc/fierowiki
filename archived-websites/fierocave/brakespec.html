Fiero Rotors Spec

Brake Rotor Service specifications in PDF format. Covers all Fiero Plus Grand Am rotors. (In IE, right click the link and select "Save Target As" for easier download to disk.)

Erratum

I'm not going to edit the PDF for this info...

1987 Fiero Service manual allows Lateral Runout = 0.005 in.
Also says Max Rotor Tickness: 0.445 in. for front, 0.50 in. for rear
This is handy for check New rotors to make sure they made them to the right thickness. Some rotors are sold needing turning before use.
