Product Part Numbers and Reviews

All product mentioned in Fiero Cave articles is reviewed and presented "as vended." This means it is the product actually purchased from store shelves. No manufacturer or distributor has paid for placement in the cave.

Why do you list so much MotorMite product?

One very simple reason. They have essentially cornered the market on many parts that would other wise be unavailable to the public in many cases. They are often the only source for many of the fasteners and other items listed besides a dealer. The red carded HELP! product line alone caries more odd little bits of stuff than I can even begin to list. All MotorMite products listed by part number have been used or verified by me to work in the application indicated.
